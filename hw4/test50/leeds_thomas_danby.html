|website = http://www.leedscitycollege.ac.uk/ }} '''Leeds Thomas Danby''' was a further education college in Leeds, West Yorkshire, England offering courses for 16-18 year-olds and adults. The college was named after the first , Captain Thomas Danby of Farnley. On the 1 April 2009, Leeds Thomas Danby merged with Park Lane College and the Leeds College of Technology to form the new ''' Leeds City College '''. The Leeds Thomas Danby site is now known as the Thomas Danby Campus of the new college. ==External links== *[http://www.leedscitycollege.ac.uk Leeds City College web site]
