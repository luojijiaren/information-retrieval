The female name '''Hildegard''' derived from the Old High German words '' hild '' (=war or battle) and ''gard'' (=protection) and means "protecting battle-maid". There were several persons in with the name '''Hildegard''': * Hildegard Behrens (1937-2009), German opera singer * Hildegard of Bingen (Saint Hildegard) * Hildegard Knef * Hildegard, wife of Charlemagne, third wife of Charlemagne * Hildegard, daughter of Louis or Matilda, daughter of Emperor Louis the Pious and Ermengarde In '''fiction''': * Hilde, or Hildegard von Krone, a fictional character from the Soul series of fighting games ==See also== * Hildegarde (disambiguation)
